Welcome to Endless Mode

Endless Mode is a single-player challenge where you battle against randomly selected Pokémon until your team faints. Your score will be recorded on the leaderboard!

How Endless Mode Works

In Endless Mode, you will face an endless stream of randomly selected Pokémon. Here's how it works:

  • Choose your team of Pokémon and your trainer name.
  • Battle against randomly selected Pokémon one at a time.
  • Each victory increases your score.
  • The game ends when all of your Pokémon faint.
  • Your final score will be added to the leaderboard.

Choosing Your Team

Before starting Endless Mode, you will select your team of Pokémon. Follow these steps:

  1. Click on a Pokémon from the list to view its details.
  2. Review its stats, type, and available moves.
  3. Click on the image of the Pokémon to open its detailed selector.
  4. Use the arrows to switch between Pokémon options.
  5. Click "Save" to confirm your selection.

Tip: Build a balanced team with complementary types to cover weaknesses!

Battle Mechanics

The battle mechanics in Endless Mode are the same as in multiplayer mode:

  • Take turns selecting moves for your Pokémon to attack the opponent.
  • Each Pokémon has a set of moves based on its type.
  • Moves consume stamina, so manage your resources wisely.
  • Victory is achieved when the opponent's Pokémon faints.

Tip: Use type advantages and special moves to maximize your chances of winning!

Scoring System

Your score in Endless Mode is based on the number of Pokémon you defeat:

  • Each defeated Pokémon adds 1 point to your score.
  • Bonus points are awarded for defeating Pokémon with type advantages.
  • Your final score is recorded on the leaderboard when your team faints.

Tip: Aim for a high score by defeating as many Pokémon as possible!

Leaderboard

The leaderboard displays the top scores in Endless Mode:

  • Your score will be added to the leaderboard if it is among the top scores.
  • Compete with other players to achieve the highest score!

Tip: Keep practicing to improve your strategy and climb the leaderboard!

Endless Mode Tips

Here are some tips to help you succeed in Endless Mode:

  • Choose Pokémon with high stamina to last longer in battles.
  • Include a mix of offensive and defensive Pokémon in your team.
  • Take advantage of type advantages to deal extra damage.
  • Manage your stamina carefully to avoid running out during critical moments.

Thank You for Playing!

Good luck in Endless Mode! Aim for the highest score and become the ultimate Pokémon Trainer!
